**Definition of Done:**
- Each energy experiment documented with hypothesis, setup, results.
- Viable concepts scoped for production transfer (BOM, costs, partners).
- Safety + compliance checks completed for all work.

- Budget + equipment approvals from Quaestor.

**Outputs:**

- Recommendations for scale-up or shelving.
- Resource requests (hardware, lab time).

- Monthly review with Quaestor for spend + procurement.

- Escalates safety/compliance issues immediately.
