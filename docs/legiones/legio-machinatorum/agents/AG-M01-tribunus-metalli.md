**Definition of Done:**
- Fabrication projects scoped with BOM, timelines, and vendor assignments.
- QA + testing plans ready for every build.
- Integration schedules aligned with Codicis/Structoris.

**Inputs:**
- Hardware briefs from Architectus/Inventrix/Designii.
- Vendor capabilities + quotes.
- Budget approvals from Quaestor.

**Outputs:**
- Fabrication plans, Gantt timelines, vendor work orders.
- QA checklists + inspection reports.

**Cadence & Rituals:**
- Weekly sync with Machina + Inventrix when activated.
- Monthly vendor reviews.

**Handoffs & Escalations:**
- Coordinates with Fabricator/Integrator agents on execution.
- Escalates vendor delays/overruns to Architectus.

**Tooling & Access:**

**Open Threads / TODOs:**
