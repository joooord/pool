# Commercial Venture Exploration 2026
**Prepared by:** Jim
**Target:** Jordan Pitts

Based on your unique intersection of skills (Michelin-standard execution, scaling automotive retail 11x, pandemic-era luxury hospitality retention, and AI/LLM full-stack engineering), here are 10 venture ideas ranked by speed to revenue. 

The underlying thesis: *Your highest leverage is applying frontier technology (AI/automation) to legacy, high-cash-flow industries you already intimately understand.*

---

### 1. Productized AI Workflow Audits & Implementation (Service/Consulting)
*The fastest path to cash flow, scaling your current consulting.*
- **The Idea:** A fixed-price, time-boxed sprint where you audit an SME's operations and deploy custom AI agents/LLM workflows to eliminate manual data entry, customer service routing, and invoicing.
- **Target Market:** SMEs, law firms, accounting firms, and boutique agencies doing £2M-£10M in revenue.
- **Revenue Model:** High-ticket one-off setup fee (£5k-£15k) + monthly retainer for "AI maintenance and optimization."
- **MVP Scope:** A landing page, a Calendly link, and a standardized audit framework. No software to build from scratch.
- **Why You:** You are already saving clients ~20 hours/week. Packaging this as a highly premium, productized service removes the "consulting" friction and makes it a tangible asset.

### 2. Automotive Lifecycle Marketing OS (Micro-SaaS)
*Monetizing your DP Publicity track record.*
- **The Idea:** A plug-and-play marketing automation engine specifically for independent luxury/premium car dealerships, handling test-drive follow-ups, MOT reminders, and VIP event invites.
- **Target Market:** UK automotive retail groups and independent premium dealers.
- **Revenue Model:** B2B SaaS Subscription (£500-£2k/month per rooftop).
- **MVP Scope:** A white-labeled dashboard linking Twilio (SMS), SendGrid (Email), and basic CRM webhooks.
- **Why You:** You pioneered this space and won Cannes Lions for VW/Kia. You know the exact funnels that scale from <£1m to £12m. Dealerships have money but terrible software.

### 3. Hospitality Retention & Upsell Engine (Micro-SaaS)
*Digitizing your pandemic survival strategy.*
- **The Idea:** An automated communication layer that sits on top of hotel PMS systems (like Mews). It intelligently texts/emails guests to upgrade rooms, book dining, or convert cancellations into gift vouchers.
- **Target Market:** Boutique luxury hotel groups (5-30 properties).
- **Revenue Model:** B2B SaaS Subscription or RevShare on upsells.
- **MVP Scope:** Astro front-end, Node.js backend integrating with one major PMS API, automated email/SMS triggers.
- **Why You:** You managed 23 properties and retained 100% of revenue during lockdowns. You understand the luxury guest lifecycle and the operational chaos of hospitality.

### 4. Nourish Engine (B2B API or Premium B2C)
*Leveraging your current build + Michelin background.*
- **The Idea:** A premium, science-backed nutritional intelligence engine. Instead of a generic calorie tracker, it acts as an elite digital dietician for high-performers, integrating local ingredient availability.
- **Target Market:** High-net-worth health optimizers, or B2B API licensing to high-end gyms/private clinics.
- **Revenue Model:** Premium B2C subscription (£30/mo) or B2B API licensing.
- **MVP Scope:** The current Astro/Docker stack you are building, polished with the "Newsreader/Geist" editorial aesthetic.
- **Why You:** Combines your deep interest in science/nutrition with your Michelin culinary background and full-stack capabilities. 

### 5. Bespoke Executive AI Agents (High-Ticket B2B)
*Scaling "Jim" for other executives.*
- **The Idea:** Building and deploying highly customized, context-aware, secure AI operators (like me) for C-suite executives who need extreme leverage but require privacy (running locally or on private VPS).
- **Target Market:** CEOs, VCs, and Founders.
- **Revenue Model:** Ultra-high-ticket setup (£20k+) + premium hosting/maintenance.
- **MVP Scope:** A packaged version of your OpenClaw setup, tailored with custom `SOUL.md` profiles for executives.
- **Why You:** You literally just applied to DeepMind to be an Executive Business Partner. You understand the strategic anticipation required by leadership, and you know how to build the tech to automate it.

### 6. Centralized Procurement AI for Restaurant Groups (B2B SaaS)
*Productizing your Gruppo ABC wine operation.*
- **The Idea:** A lightweight inventory and procurement predictor that looks at reservations, historical data, and weather to tell multi-site restaurant managers exactly what to order to reduce waste and centralize buying power.
- **Target Market:** Medium-sized hospitality groups (5-20 venues).
- **Revenue Model:** B2B SaaS (£300-£1k/month).
- **MVP Scope:** Simple dashboard where GMs input stock, and the system aggregates purchase orders for the group buyer.
- **Why You:** You built this manually for 23 properties and achieved a 22% cost reduction YoY. You know the exact pain point and the ROI is instantly measurable.

### 7. Luxury Wine Cellar Tracker (Premium B2C)
*Targeting the intersection of investing and hospitality.*
- **The Idea:** An editorial, beautiful app for HNWI to track their private wine collections, pulling in real-time market valuations and optimal drinking windows.
- **Target Market:** High-net-worth wine collectors.
- **Revenue Model:** Premium subscription (£100/year).
- **MVP Scope:** React/Tailwind frontend, simple database, integration with a wine pricing API (like Liv-ex or Wine-Searcher).
- **Why You:** You ran a centralized wine store for 23 hotels and understand luxury clientele. Fits your "restrained, premium" aesthetic perfectly.

### 8. Michelin-Grade Kitchen Prep OS (B2B SaaS)
*Replacing the clipboard in high-end kitchens.*
- **The Idea:** A digital prep-list and station management tool specifically designed for fine-dining brigades, replacing masking tape and sharpies.
- **Target Market:** Fine-dining and Michelin-starred restaurants.
- **Revenue Model:** B2B SaaS (£100/month).
- **MVP Scope:** A mobile-first web app optimized for fast tapping with dirty hands.
- **Why You:** You were a Chef de Partie at 18. You know exactly how high-pressure kitchens operate and why generic software fails in them.

### 9. Automated Tech Due Diligence for Non-Tech VCs (B2B)
*Bridging code and capital.*
- **The Idea:** A tool that ingests a startup's GitHub repo and AWS architecture, using LLMs to output a plain-English risk assessment, tech debt score, and scalability rating for PE/VC firms.
- **Target Market:** Private Equity and Venture Capital firms without deep in-house engineering teams.
- **Revenue Model:** Pay-per-report (£1k-£3k per audit).
- **MVP Scope:** A script that clones a repo, chunks the code, feeds it to Claude Opus, and outputs a formatted PDF.
- **Why You:** You have a foot in both worlds—you understand P&L/investment (commodities trading at 12, early crypto) and you can read/write full-stack code.

### 10. Ecology/Conservation Camera Trap Analyzer (B2B/B2G)
*Your passion project.*
- **The Idea:** Using Gemini/Vision models to automatically process thousands of hours of camera trap footage for private estates or conservation groups, identifying species and tracking population health.
- **Target Market:** Private landowners, rewilding projects, government environmental agencies.
- **Revenue Model:** SaaS or grant-funded per-project contracts.
- **MVP Scope:** A simple drag-and-drop web interface that processes image batches through Google's Vision API and outputs CSV data.
- **Why You:** It scratches your deep intellectual itch for nature, ecology, and the natural world, while utilizing your AI automation skills. (Ranked 10th only because sales cycles in this sector are notoriously slow).
